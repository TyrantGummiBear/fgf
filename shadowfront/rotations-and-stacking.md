# Rotations & Stacking

Control mode lives or dies on **when to stack** and **when to rotate**. ShadowFront is the same — but remember, **numbers in the circle** win captures, not raw fleet power.

---

## Stacking (Default)

**Stack = send enough fleets into one capture circle to outnumber the enemy.**

### When to stack
- Coordinator calls a vault target
- Enemy has more numbers in the circle
- We're contesting a countdown or bonus window — see [Bonus Points](bonus-points.md)
- Tower is down (or about to fall) and we need bodies in the circle

### How to stack
1. Same vault, same timing
2. All fleets **inside the capture circle** — see [Fight in the Circle](fight-on-the-point.md)
3. Remove enemy players in the circle; don't spread damage outside the zone

**Rule:** Five average fleets in the circle beat one strong fleet outside it.

---

## Rotations

**Rotate = leave your current vault and stack a new capture circle as a group.**

Only rotate when the coordinator calls it or the situation is clearly lost.

### Good rotation triggers
| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| We can't win the number fight here | Rotate to winnable vault |
| Coordinator calls **"Rotate to [vault]"** | Move immediately |
| Enemy over-stacked elsewhere | Rotate if current vault is lost cause |
| Late game, leader vault matters more | Abandon low-value vault, stack numbers on target |

### Bad rotation triggers
| Situation | Why it's wrong |
|-----------|----------------|
| Leaving while we still have number advantage | Capture stops |
| Chasing one ship outside the circle | They keep the count |
| Solo rotate without call | Split the stack |
| One strong fleet "handles it" alone | Numbers beat power |

---

## Split vs. Stack Decision

```
Do we have more numbers in the circle right now?
├── YES → Stay. Remove stragglers. Finish capture.
└── NO  → Can we get 2+ more fleets in the circle soon?
          ├── YES → Call for stack
          └── NO  → Coordinator rotates or calls Deny
```

---

## Home Vault vs. Away Vault

- **Home vault** — minimum bodies **in the circle** at all times
- **Attack vault** — full stack, win the count
- **Contested vault** — whoever has more in the circle captures

Never leave home circle empty while enemy is nearby.

---

## The 2-Squad Model (Simple)

| Squad | Job |
|-------|-----|
| **Alpha** | Main stack — coordinator's vault, win numbers |
| **Bravo** | Home hold OR reinforce Alpha on call |

Bravo doesn't roam. They hold a circle or respond to **"Need stack on [vault]"**.

---

## CoD Control Parallel

| Control habit | ShadowFront habit |
|---------------|-------------------|
| "They're taking B!" | Stack numbers in B's circle |
| "Stop feeding B alone" | One fleet can't win the count |
| "Trade on the hill" | Die in the circle so teammate holds count |
| "Play the point" | Play the number fight, not damage stats |

---

## Summary

- **Default:** Stack one circle, outnumber the enemy inside it
- **Rotate:** On call or when count is unwinnable
- **Never:** Spread thin with nobody in any circle
