# Common Mistakes

Fix these and we win more matches — **without** needing stronger ships.

---

## 1. Fighting Outside the Capture Circle

**Symptom:** Good damage, losing captures anyway.

**Fix:** [Fight in the Circle](fight-on-the-point.md) — only fleets **inside the circle** count.

---

## 2. Assuming Strong Ships Win the Point

**Symptom:** One whale fights alone; enemy stacks three in the circle and captures.

**Fix:** **Numbers beat power.** Stack bodies in the circle.

---

## 3. Not Targeting Enemies in the Circle

**Symptom:** We kill ships outside the zone; their capture keeps ticking.

**Fix:** Remove enemy players **inside the circle first** — **lowest HP first** to drop numbers fast. [Advanced Tactics](advanced-tactics.md)

---

## 4. Spread Fire / Ignoring Focus Calls

**Symptom:** Everyone shooting a different target; nobody drops; capture stalls.

**Fix:** **Focus lowest HP in circle.** **Focus whale** when we **have numbers**. **Avoid whale** when we don't — kite if they target you.

---

## 5. Our Whale Isolated in Circle

**Symptom:** Enemy focuses our whale easy pick; we lose our anchor.

**Fix:** **Stack on our whale** inside the circle. Bodies on top = harder to target.

---

## 6. Feeding the Enemy Whale

**Symptom:** Whale enters; we're outnumbered; everyone stands and trades — we die, count unchanged.

**Fix:** **Avoid whale** when we lack numbers. **Drag out when targeted** — if they chase, bait via [Micro](micro.md).

---

## 7. Burning Tower While Outnumbered

**Symptom:** Tower falls, enemies still have numbers, instant recapture.

**Fix:** Clear enough players that tower drop lets **us** win the count. Exception: **"Tower first"** when big incoming.

---

## 8. Chasing Enemies Out of the Circle

**Symptom:** Enemy runs; we follow for the kill; they keep count with ships still inside.

**Fix:** **Don't chase runners** — hold the circle. See [Advanced Tactics](advanced-tactics.md).

**Opposite mistake:** You're bait but stay in circle eating focus. **Drag one ship out** from the left panel — [Micro](micro.md).

---

## 9. Moving Whole Stack Instead of One Ship

**Symptom:** Bait or avoid play pulls entire fleet out of circle; count collapses.

**Fix:** [Micro](micro.md) — drag **only the targeted ship** from the left panel. Rest hold count.

---

## 10. Solo Pushing a Stacked Circle

**Symptom:** One fleet vs. four in the circle — no contest.

**Fix:** Wait for stack. You need numbers, not hero plays.

---

## 11. Splitting Across Every Vault

**Symptom:** Nobody wins any number fight.

**Fix:** [Rotations & Stacking](rotations-and-stacking.md) — one main stack, one home hold.

---

## 12. Forcing Flip When Deny Is Correct

**Symptom:** Can't win count in time; we throw HP anyway.

**Fix:** [Bonus Points](bonus-points.md) — **Deny** when capture isn't winnable.

---

## 13. Support Never Re-enters Circle

**Symptom:** Nodes grabbed, vault understaffed, count lost.

**Fix:** Nodes → straight back into the circle.

---

## 14. Damage Farming Over Objectives

**Symptom:** Top damage, never in the circle when capture ends.

**Fix:** **Count in circle > damage dealt.**
