# ShadowFront Bonus Points

Two approaches by countdown timing. Both assume normal [fight priority](fight-on-the-point.md) — unless noted below.

**A — Steal:** Enemy mid-countdown, we can **win the number fight** in the circle. Remove their players, flip the count, take the bonus. Only commit if we can hold numbers after.

**B — Deny:** Too late to win capture ourselves. Break the countdown at the last safe moment so they can't recover before the window closes. Strip points; don't overcommit to holding the circle.

**Exception:** When denying, we may **not** win the circle — that's fine. Goal is preventing bonus, not flipping. Still remove players if it breaks their countdown; don't force a full capture.

**Rule:** Can we win numbers + hold in time? Yes → Steal. No → Deny.

Calls: "Flip" = A | "Deny" = B
