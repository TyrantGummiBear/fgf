# Squad Roles

> **Work in progress.** This doc is a living draft — we're still working through how we assign and run roles. Treat the labels below as a starting framework, not final doctrine.

Everyone needs a job before match start. All roles share one rule: **win the number fight inside the capture circle.**

---

## Roles vs. Flexibility

**Specific roles vs. flexibility and situational awareness is the difference between wins and losses.**

- **Roles** give clarity before the fight — who holds home, who stacks, who flexes
- **Flexibility** is what you do when the match doesn't follow the plan — vault flips, whale shows up, bonus window opens, you're targeted
- **Situational awareness** is reading the moment: count in circle, HP dropping, coordinator call (or no call yet), flip vs deny

Rigid players lose when the situation changes. Players with no role lose when nobody holds the circle. **We need both** — a default assignment *and* the judgment to break it when the game demands it.

This section will evolve as we test what works for our guild. Feedback from matches welcome.

---

## Roles

| Role | Job | Priority target |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| **Stack / Capture** | Outnumber enemy in the circle | Enemy players **in the circle** |
| **Home / Hold** | Keep our count on home vault | Anyone entering our circle |
| **Deny / Flex** | Break enemy bonus windows | Players in circle, or deny-only — [Bonus Points](bonus-points.md) |
| **Support** | Repair via Short Awakening nodes | Nodes, then back into the circle |

---

## Stack / Capture

- Wait for stack if coordinator says so — **numbers matter**
- **Lowest HP in circle first** — drop numbers fast
- **Group focus enemy whales** when they enter **and we have numbers**
- **Avoid whales** when outnumbered — move if targeted, bait out if they chase
- If tower blocks capture: clear enough players that tower drop = we win the count
- **Exception:** Big incoming → tower first if coordinator calls it

**Bad habit:** Trading with a whale while losing the count. Or ignoring a whale when we have numbers and could burn it.

---

## Home / Hold

- Home is lost when enemy has more numbers in our circle
- Focus **lowest HP in circle** to flip count quickly
- **Stack on our whale** if holding home — don't leave them isolated

---

## Deny / Flex

- **Flip:** Win numbers, steal bonus — full [fight priority](fight-on-the-point.md)
- **Deny:** Can't win capture — break countdown, prevent bonus, don't overcommit to holding circle
- Wait for coordinator call before switching modes

---

## Support

- Grab Short Awakening nodes, return to circle immediately
- Support fills a number slot — get back in the zone fast

---

## Pre-Match Assignment

```
Alpha (Stack):  [names]  → primary vault, win count
Bravo (Home):   [names]  → home circle hold
Flex:           [names]  → deny / reinforce
Support:        [names]  → nodes + re-enter circle
```

Unlisted? Default to **Stack**, follow vault calls.

---

## Role Switching Mid-Match

Roles are **defaults**. Mid-match, play what the situation needs — with awareness, not freelancing.

| Signal | Flex play |
|--------|-----------|
| Home empty, enemy walking in | Drop stack role — hold home |
| Bonus window, we're short on numbers | Flex → stack, not deny solo |
| You're targeted, team has count | Micro out — [Micro](micro.md) |
| Coordinator reassigns | Switch immediately |

Coordinator calls (when we have comms):

| Call | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **"Bravo reinforce Alpha"** | Home squad stacks for numbers |
| **"Flex deny [vault]"** | Deny bonus — capture optional |
| **"Tower first [vault]"** | Priority shift — drop tower |
| **"[Name] hold home"** | Stay in home circle |

No call? **Read the circle** — who has numbers, who's losing HP, what vault matters right now.

---

## Summary

Your role = **default vault and job**. [Fight in the Circle](fight-on-the-point.md) = **how**. **Situational awareness** = when to stay in role vs. flex — that's what separates wins from losses.
