# Whales & Large Fights

General PvP doctrine for fighting **with** guild whales in open engagements — not ShadowFront circle rules.

Read [ShadowFront Advanced Tactics](../shadowfront/advanced-tactics.md) when the fight **is** on a vault circle.

---

## Fight around whales

**Our whales are the center of the fight.** The guild fights **around** them — not spread across the map, not solo hunting on the flanks.

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Stay in **weapon and support range** of our whale | Drift 20km away chasing stragglers |
| Let engagements happen **in the whale's space** | Turn a fleet fight into isolated 1v1s |
| Move **with** our whale when they push or peel | Leave the whale behind to "clean up" |
| Bring the fight **to the whale** (yours or theirs) | Fight somewhere our whale can't help or be helped |

Our whale is the **anchor**. Smaller ships are the **pile around that anchor** — peel, focus fire, and eliminate threats from that position.

**Call:** **"On [whale name]"** — form on our whale; this is where we fight.

---

## Large-scale PvP — eliminate threats first

In big fleet fights, **remove what can kill us before you farm what's easy**.

| Threat | Why it matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Enemy whales on our whales | Can delete our anchor with no support |
| Focus fire on our individual members | Drops our numbers while whales trade |
| Enemy whales roaming free | Can pick any target — highest burst on the field |

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Kill or drive off **dangerous** targets first | Chase a low-HP straggler while a whale kills our stack |
| Keep our whales alive and supported | Let whales 1v1 while the rest of the fleet spreads out |
| **Fight around the whale** — threats near our anchor first | Chase kills far from our whale |
| Switch to next **threat** after one drops | Tunnel one dead ship while their whale resets |

**Rule:** Threats first, cleanup second.

---

## Our whales never fight enemy whales alone

A whale **dueling another whale with no support** is how we lose anchors for free. **Our whales should never need to fight enemy whales alone.**

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Move to the whale fight **as soon as it starts** | Wait to see who wins the 1v1 |
| Stack and fire with our whale | Send our whale in solo "to test them" |
| Call **"Stack on [our whale]"** / **"Help [name]"** | Assume the whale can tank two enemy whales |

If our whale is engaged with an enemy whale, **that is the main fight** until we have numbers on it or our whale is safe.

---

## Priority order when whales are involved

When whales are on the field, use this order **every time**:

```
1. Help OUR whales     → join, stack, peel what's on them
2. Focus enemy whales  → burn whales threatening our whales
3. Individual attackers → pick off whoever is killing our smaller ships
```

### 1 — Help our whales

- Get bodies **on our whale** — overlap, stack, stay in weapon range
- Peel fire off our whale: lowest-HP enemies **on our whale** first if you can't reach their whale yet
- Our whale holds the line; **everyone else joins the pile**

**Call:** **"Stack on [name]"** · **"Help [name]"** · **"Whale needs help"**

### 2 — Focus enemy whales (to help our whales)

Once we're on our whale, **group focus the enemy whale(s) in that fight**.

- Don't spread — **same target** as our whale's fight
- Drop their whale → our whale lives → we win the exchange
- Enemy whale forced out → back to step 3

**Call:** **"Focus whale [name]"** — all fire on that whale until down or breaking.

### 3 — Individual attackers

After the whale brawl is stable (our whale safe, enemy whale down or routed):

- Kill ships **actively attacking our members** — especially anyone focus-firing our whale or our healers/peel
- Then **lowest HP** among remaining threats still shooting at us
- Ignore runners and stragglars until **no one on our side is in immediate danger**

**Call:** **"Kill [name]"** · **"Peel [name] off [ally]"**

## Fighting around whales (non-whale players)

You are not filler — you **make the whale fight winnable** by staying **on and around** our whale.

| Job | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **Hold the anchor** | Default position is **on our whale** — that's where the fight is |
| **Join the pile** | When whales collide, be **in** the brawl — not orbiting far away |
| **Focus with the whale** | Same enemy whale / same attackers as the coordinator calls |
| **Don't solo hunt** | No chasing a lone frigate while our whale dies in a 1v2 |
| **Call early** | **"Enemy whale on [our whale]"** beats **"We lost [our whale]"** |

### Quick decision tree

```
Where should I be?
├── Default → On our whale — fight around them
└── Whale in fight with enemy whale?
    ├── YES → Stack on our whale → Focus enemy whale → peel individual attackers
    └── NO  → Stay on whale; kill threats in range; don't range out alone
```

## Common mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Fleet spread out, whale alone in the middle of enemies | **Fight around whales** — pile on our anchor |
| Our whale starts a whale 1v1 alone | **Never** — fleet moves with the whale |
| Whole fleet chases one small ship | Threats first — whale on our side > straggler |
| Everyone shoots different targets in whale fight | **Focus enemy whale** with our whale |
| Ignoring ships picking off our stack | Step 3 — kill whoever is attacking us individually |
| Applying ShadowFront "avoid whale" here blindly | Open PvP: **support our whale**, don't leave them solo |

## Summary

| Principle | Guild standard |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Position** | **Fight around our whales** — they are the anchor |
| **Scale** | Large PvP — **eliminate threats first** |
| **Whale duels** | **Our whales never fight enemy whales alone** |
| **Order** | Help our whales → focus enemy whales → kill individual attackers |
| **Your job** | Stay on the whale, stack when called, don't range out alone |
